Quick access to Bacteria Identified by Automated Systems (BIAS) on PubMed

By Faculty of Medical Technology, Mahidol University, Thailand

Our Goal

Rationale and Goal: More and more uncommon medically-important bacteria have been identified and published on PubMed thanks to the advanced automated systems. This project aims to collect and extract the information that could be useful for a medical microbiologist who may need information to validate the identification of uncommon bacteria. The database is updated monthly to monitor newly reported bacteria. It currently stores information of bacteria that were possibly identified by VITEK MS, VITEK 2 or Biotyper.
